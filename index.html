Montessori Program

In the Montessori tradition, each class is a prepared environment alive with a wealth of intriguing materials. The child moves at his/her own pace through all areas of the room â€“ Practical Life, Sensorial, Language, Mathematics and Cultural.

Art Class

In order to encourage creativity, the art program involves exploration with a wide variety of media, techniques, and colour, and reinforces Montessori principles. Children, in small groups, are scheduled to participate in art activities in the art room on a once weekly basis.

French

French is introduced to all the children weekly. The emphasis is on vocabulary building through themes, songs, puppets and conversation. Additional French time is provided for the kindergarten students.

Grow their creative confidence

Each creative activity prompt is an opportunity for your child to embrace a new challenge. Through hands-on learning, your child is more likely to remain engaged and to build upon their creative problem-solving skills.

Prepare them to thrive

Our child centered classroom gives your little ones the opportunity to explore the foundation for language, math and sciences through practical life skills, sensorial learning and child led lessons.
